Newport Jazz Festival 65'

This poster is a recreation of the iconic Newport Jazz Festival '65. Created using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, I chose to feature legendary jazz musicians Frank Sinatra and Count Basie, who performed at the festival. The color scheme is designed to evoke a vibrant, jazz-inspired atmosphere, while the font selection adds a celebratory touch. I experimented with various text placements, such as having text emerge from a trumpet, positioning the 'afternoon' and 'evening' texts oppositely, and placing concert details in the right corner. Additionally, I manipulated the background to give transparency to the instruments and music sheets, and I applied an inner glow effect to make certain texts pop.

A Constant Fear of Uncertainty Back Cover

This poster, designed as the back cover for my book titled "A Constant Fear of Uncertainty," was crafted using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. It aims to visually capture the essence of fear, highlighted by a hand emerging from a grayscale background under a moonlit sky, adding a touch of mystery. To enhance this eerie atmosphere, I overlaid a dark background and applied a drop shadow to the text. The various hands along the sides are intended to intensify the mysterious vibe, emphasizing their diversity. The book itself delves into my personal fears, with this artwork reflecting its core themes.

Mycellium Project

This poster illustrates the concept of Mycelium, showcasing its mushroom-like roots that intertwine with trees and spread extensively. It also symbolizes connections, represented by groups of people linked to the intertwining roots, highlighting the interconnected nature of both the natural world and human relationships.

Reminiscence Collection

This project showcases a video game archive website that catalogues over 170 video games. It was inspired by my earlier work, the Reminiscence Collection, which is a poster and books detailing video game information. This website serves as my senior capstone project for Virginia Commonwealth University. It is designed to be responsive and accessible, featuring login and signup pages for enhanced user interaction.
