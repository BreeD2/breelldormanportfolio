Mycelium Project

This poster was created using Adobe Illustrator and illustrates the concept of Mycelium, showcasing its mushroom-like roots that intertwine with trees and spread extensively. It also symbolizes connections, represented by groups of people linked to the intertwining roots, highlighting the interconnected nature of both the natural world and human relationships.

Anxiety & Its Impact on School Systems

This poster provides an overview of anxiety and its effects on educational systems. It explains what anxiety is, the different types of anxiety, and the causes of school-related anxiety. I chose to explore this topic because I experienced severe anxiety as a child, which made me too nervous to speak aloud and often led me to communicate in whispers. While I have made significant progress in managing my anxiety, I believe it's an important subject to discuss. The project was crafted using Adobe InDesign for layout purposes, and I used Adobe Photoshop to alter the colors in the images.

Newport Jazz Festival 65'

This poster is a recreation of the iconic Newport Jazz Festival '65. Created using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, I chose to feature legendary jazz musicians Frank Sinatra and Count Basie, who performed at the festival. The color scheme is designed to evoke a vibrant, jazz-inspired atmosphere, while the font selection adds a celebratory touch. I experimented with various text placements, such as having text emerge from a trumpet, positioning the 'afternoon' and 'evening' texts oppositely, and placing concert details in the right corner. Additionally, I manipulated the background to give transparency to the instruments and music sheets, and I applied an inner glow effect to make certain texts pop.
